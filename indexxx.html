Ángela Castillo Ferrero Jewelry / Fashion Accessories designer
Based in Barcelona
               

castillofereroangela@gmail.com

           

Ángela is a designer focused on creating narratives through material culture. Her work explores the intersection between digital craftsmanship and traditional techniques, creating fashion accessories with a unique approach.
Throughout her career, she has worked on innovative parametric jewelry projects and has participated in international design exhibitions. Her fascination with materials and their transformation drives her to constantly seek new forms of expression.
