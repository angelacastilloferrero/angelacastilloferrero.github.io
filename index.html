Ángela Castillo Ferrero Jewelry / Fashion Accessories designer
Based in Barcelona
castilloferreroangela@gmail.com

A hybrid professional, an engineer applying design principles ⇄ designer implementing engineering methodology (I'm super quiet too, like a Prius). Fully mastered end-to-end product development, from initial conceptualization to final realization.

My expertise is rooted in jewelry design, working with noble and precious materials like gems, silver, 18K gold, semi-precious gemstones, lab-grown diamonds… My approach is strategic, driven by cost orientation, commercial vision, global brand understanding, and a relentless eye for detail.

Beyond designing for remarkable brands, I also partner with emerging labels, offering creative guidance. My work is diverse; from fashion brands to collaborating with artistic talents or developing custom pieces.
