keyboard

product/software

A software/hardware combination remains a custom-built QWERTY keyboard into musical scales, taking both the tactile qualities from mechanical keyboard switches and the expressive freedom and customizability from MIDI piano keyboards. Designed specifically for composers in the making, and for those who enjoy music production, the tool works as a bridge between typists and musicians. The tool provides a fresh means of inputs for existing composers stack in melodic ruts as well as open up the opportunity for experienced typists to become QWERTY virtuoso pianists.
